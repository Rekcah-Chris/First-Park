About Us


Vehicle parking system is a system used to manage the parking of vehicles, typically in a large parking facility such as parking lots. This can include a variety of technology such as reservation of parking lots. The goal of a vehicle parking system is to make it easy for drivers to find and secure a parking spot and to make efficient use of the available parking spaces.

The vehicle parking system for Kenya coast national polytechnic is currently done manually by security guards to allocate parking slots which are free. This is a crucial problem of vehicle parking, for one to park a vehicle the security guards have to look for all the lanes, hence this involves a lot manual labor with the use of vehicle parking system for Kenya coast national polytechnic, the security guard will be able to allocate a free parking lot for a vehicle in a faster way.

The vehicle parking system will track the entry and exit of cars, maintain a listing of cars within the parking lots, and determine if the parking lot is full or not. it will enable the time management and control of vehicle using number plate recognitions.
