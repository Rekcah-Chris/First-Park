Parking Information

Welcome to Kenya Coast National Polytechnic parking management system, designed to provide a seamless and convenient parking experience for students, staff, and visitors. Our parking system is equipped with advanced technology to ensure efficient parking management and enhanced security.
Our parking lot is located within the Polytechnic premises, easily accessible from the main entrance. We offer both covered and uncovered parking spaces to cater to your specific parking needs.
The parking lot is open 24/7, allowing for flexible parking options for all users.
We offer both cash and mobile payments through M-Pesa to make parking transactions fast and secure. Our rates are affordable, ensuring that you get value for your money.
Our parking lot is monitored 24/7 by a team of security personnel and CCTV cameras, ensuring the safety of your vehicle at all times.
We have designated parking spaces for people with disabilities, ensuring that everyone has access to convenient parking.
To find our parking lot, follow the signs from the main entrance of Kenya Coast National Polytechnic.
We strive to make parking at Kenya Coast National Polytechnic a hassle-free experience, and we look forward to serving you.

Lot Information

Kenya Coast National Polytechnic has a well-maintained parking lot that is designed to provide ample space for visitors and staff members. Our parking lot is conveniently located near the main entrance of the campus, making it easy for everyone to access.

    Parking Lot Features:

  1. The parking lot is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, including CCTV cameras and an automated payment system.:
  2. Our parking lot has designated parking spaces for visitors, staff, and people with disabilities
  3. We have ample space for motorcycles and bicycles
  4. The parking lot is well-lit to ensure the safety of our users, especially during the night

Our lot is designed to accommodate different types of vehicles, including cars, motorcycles, and bicycles.
Parking is available on a first-come, first-served basis.
The parking fee is determined by the duration of parking.
Our parking fees are affordable and competitive with other parking lots in the area.
To find our parking lot, follow the signs from the main entrance of Kenya Coast National Polytechnic.
We are committed to providing a safe and convenient parking experience for all users, and we look forward to serving you.
